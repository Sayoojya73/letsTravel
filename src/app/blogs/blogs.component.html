DISCOVER THE STUNNING BALEARIC ISLAND

MENORCA

Although it may principally be viewed as a summer holiday island - a place to relax, top up the tan, and eat well - the truth is that Menorca isn’t quite what you may be thinking of when ‘Spanish holiday destination’ comes to mind. Sure, there’s coastal resorts and swimming pools and luxury hotels and holiday homes and tourists and beaches - all the elements you’d expect - but this is a quieter place where a balance, delicate and fragile though it may be, is being sought.

About a fifth of the size of neighbouring Mallorca, instead of the brash concrete, noise, and hedonism you find elsewhere, the words which come to mind for us most when we think of Menorca are: undeveloped, understated, ruggedly rural and remoteness.
